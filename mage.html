About Me

I am Velhurst Stallhein, an Archmage from the mystical lands of Eldoria. For over two decades, I have devoted myself to mastering the arcane arts, particularly in the manipulation of elemental forces and the bending of time itself. My deep knowledge of ancient magic makes me both feared and respected, though I prefer a life of solitude where I can study and refine my craft. Calm and calculating, I rely on my intellect to overcome challenges, and I believe that knowledge is the true source of power. As a protector of Eldoria, I uphold a strong sense of duty, always ready to defend my home from looming threats.
