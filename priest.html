About Me

Greetings, I am Selene Archfield, a Priestess of Silvermoon Sanctuary. Guided by my unwavering faith, I have committed my life to the service of others through divine magic. My power lies in the art of healing and offering protection to those in need. Compassion drives every decision I make, and I believe that light and love can overcome any darkness. Despite the struggles the world faces, I stand firm in my belief that kindness and devotion can bring hope to even the most desperate situations. When I am not tending to the wounded, I offer guidance and solace to those seeking spiritual peace.
